What You'll Learn

A Complete Journey From Zero to Garba

Our curriculum is designed to be joyful, progressive, and performance-ready.

🎵
Rhythm & Beat

Master the 3-beat and 6-beat Garba cycles. Learn clapping patterns (taali) and feel the music in your body.

💃
Footwork & Steps

From basic 2-step to Dodhiyu, Teen Taali, and advanced Raas patterns. Build speed and precision.

🙌
Expression & Mudras

Learn hand gestures and facial expressions that bring Garba alive with emotion and grace.

🥁
Dandiya Raas

Partner and group dandiya patterns for Navratri. Safe technique for competitive circles.

🎭
Performance Prep

Stage presence, costume guidance, and rehearsals for Navratri performances.

🏅
Certification

Receive a Bliss Out Dance Studio certificate of completion upon finishing the workshop.
